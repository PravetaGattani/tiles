Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about careers at Square Feet

What is the hiring process like?

Our hiring process typically includes resume screening, a phone interview, an in-person interview, and sometimes a skills assessment, depending on the position. We aim to make the process thorough yet efficient, usually completing it within 2-3 weeks.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we offer internship programs for students and recent graduates in various departments, including sales, marketing, design, and operations. These internships provide valuable industry experience and can sometimes lead to full-time positions.

What training will I receive when I join?

All new employees undergo a comprehensive orientation program to familiarize them with our company culture, products, and processes. Additionally, role-specific training is provided to equip you with the necessary skills and knowledge. We also offer ongoing professional development opportunities throughout your career with us.

What career growth opportunities are available?

We believe in promoting from within and supporting our employees' career aspirations. Regular performance reviews provide opportunities for advancement, and we encourage cross-departmental movement for those looking to expand their skill sets. Many of our senior team members started in entry-level positions.

What benefits do you offer?

We offer a competitive benefits package that includes health insurance, retirement benefits, paid time off, performance bonuses, and employee discounts on our products. We also provide opportunities for professional development and training.
