Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Our experienced team ensures proper installation according to industry standards, ensuring longevity and optimal performance of our products.

What is the typical lead time for orders?

Most in-stock items are available immediately. For special orders or custom products, lead times typically range from 2-4 weeks depending on the manufacturer and specifications. We'll provide a specific timeline when you place your order.

Do you provide samples of your products?

Yes, we understand the importance of seeing and feeling the products before making a decision. You can visit our showroom to view our entire collection, or we can arrange for samples to be sent to your location for a nominal fee.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase for unused, unopened products in their original packaging. Custom orders and cut materials are non-returnable. Please contact us for specific details or special circumstances.

Do you offer design consultation services?

Yes, our design specialists are available to help you select the right products for your space. Whether you're renovating your home or working on a commercial project, we can provide expert advice on design, materials, and functionality.
