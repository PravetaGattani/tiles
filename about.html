Our Story

Square Feet was incorporated in 2010 with a vision to redefine luxury in interior spaces. Headquartered in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, we have grown to become a trusted name in premium tiles and sanitary solutions.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality products that blend modern style with traditional craftsmanship. Over the years, we have expanded our collection to include a wide range of designer floor tiles, wall tiles, bathroom and kitchen tiles, sanitary ware, granites, and CP fittings.

Today, we serve a diverse clientele including homeowners, interior designers, architects, and builders who seek premium tiling and sanitary solutions for their residential and commercial spaces. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality products and exceptional services that transform spaces into elegant, functional environments. We are committed to understanding our customers' needs and providing tailored solutions that exceed their expectations.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of premium tiles and sanitary solutions in Central India, recognized for our quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach. We aim to be the trusted partner for all interior needs.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Innovation, and Customer Satisfaction form the core of our business philosophy. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, suppliers, and community.
